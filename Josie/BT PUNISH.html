Upon executing a successful sidestep/walk, the optimal or maximum damage punish for your character is pretty important, here's Josie's

However, characters such as Xiayou with a BT (back-turned) parry may escape this

Regarding this, you can use u/f+3+4 (knees that cannot be parried). Whilst guaranteed, it requires a sacrifice of damage as this is not a launch

Alternatively, against regular characters, you have the universally guaranteed mid option: 3,f+3, if you are afraid of someone ducking whilst BT.

Additionally, a successful parry read/prediction can be punished with CD 2, in the place of d/f+2, as it will whiff punish the parry
